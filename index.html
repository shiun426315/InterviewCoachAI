問題背景

The Problem

HR 新人培訓，
三個核心挑戰

Training New HR Interviewers
Comes With Three Hard Problems

半導體製程人才稀缺，招募品質直接影響產線效能。然而 HR 新人的面試技巧培訓，長期依賴師徒制與實戰試錯，難以標準化、也難以追蹤成效。

Skilled semiconductor talent is scarce, and hiring quality feeds directly into fab performance. Yet new HR interviewers are still trained the old way — shadowing a mentor and learning by trial and error on real candidates — a method that's nearly impossible to standardize or measure.

01

缺乏安全的練習場域

No Safe Place to Practice

新人通常直接面對真實應徵者，一旦問話失當，不僅影響候選人體驗，也可能錯失優秀人才。

New interviewers usually learn on real candidates. A poorly handled question doesn't just hurt the candidate's experience — it can cost the company a strong hire.

02

行為信號難以自學

Behavioral Signals Aren't Self-Taught

候選人的答案迴避、矛盾陳述、情緒底色等信號，仰賴大量經驗才能識別，新人往往無從察覺。

Spotting deflection, inconsistency, or an emotional undertone in an answer takes years of accumulated experience. New interviewers simply haven't built that instinct yet.

03

培訓成效無法量化

Progress Is Impossible to Quantify

師徒式培訓難以追蹤成長曲線，培訓主管無法客觀掌握每位新人的技能落差與改善方向。

Mentorship-based training leaves no growth curve to track. Training managers have no objective way to see where each new hire is falling short — or whether they're improving at all.

Observe Mode
Watch a Full Interview, Played Out by AI

選定候選人履歷後，系統自動執行 AI HR Agent 與 AI 應徵者的完整語音對話。HR 新人以旁觀者角色聆聽標準問話流程、觀察追問時機，並在對話結束後取得行為信號摘要。

Pick a candidate profile, and the system runs a complete voice interview between an AI HR Agent and an AI candidate. New interviewers watch from the sidelines — hearing how a standard question flow unfolds and when a good follow-up lands — then receive a summary of every behavioral signal the conversation surfaced.

  • 雙 Agent 語音對話，涵蓋系統定義的必問主題清單
  • A two-agent voice conversation covering every topic the system requires
  • 即時逐字稿同步顯示，標示說話角色
  • Live transcript synced to the audio, labeled by speaker
  • 對話結束後自動產出行為信號摘要，標示出現輪次
  • A behavioral-signal summary generated automatically at the end, marked to the exact round it appeared in
  • 信號類型涵蓋：答案轉移、框架式回答、一致性矛盾、情緒底色
  • Covers signal types including answer deflection, scripted framing, internal inconsistency, and emotional undertone

練習模式
親自提問，取得量化評分

Practice Mode
Run the Interview Yourself, Get Scored

HR 新人扮演面試官，對麥克風即時提問，AI 應徵者依據真實履歷生成回答。面試結束後取得 7 面向量化評分報告，以及「信號感知對照」——揭示哪些行為信號未被察覺。

The trainee takes the interviewer's seat and asks questions live, by voice, while an AI candidate answers in character based on a real resume. Afterward, they receive a scored report across seven dimensions, plus a signal-awareness comparison — showing exactly which behavioral cues they missed.

  • 語音即時辨識（Azure STT），自然說話無需手動操作
  • Real-time speech recognition (Azure STT) — just talk, no manual controls
  • AI 應徵者依履歷設計，具一致性格與答題風格
  • The AI candidate is built from the resume, with a consistent personality and way of answering
  • 7 面向分項評分，每項附具體說明與改善建議
  • Seven scored dimensions, each with a specific note and a suggestion for improvement
  • 每次練習結果自動存檔，可追蹤新人成長歷程
  • Every session is saved automatically, building a visible growth curve over time

新人認證

Certification

達標即可申請
HR 面試職能認證

Hit the Bar,
Earn HR Interview Certification

練習模式不只是訓練工具，更與正式認證機制連結。新人完成達標練習後，可向培訓主管申請認證，將面試職能納入個人職涯紀錄。

Practice Mode isn't just a training tool — it feeds directly into a formal certification path. Once a trainee clears the bar, they can apply to their training manager for certification, adding a documented interview competency to their career record.

1

完成 3 次練習並達標

Complete Three Qualifying Sessions

每次練習取得系統評分，3 次平均總分達到認證門檻（由培訓主管設定）即符合申請資格。

Each session produces a score. Once the average across three sessions clears the threshold set by the training manager, the trainee is eligible to apply.

2

系統自動彙整練習紀錄

The System Compiles the Record

3 次練習的逐字稿、評分報告與信號感知對照，自動整合為認證申請包，供培訓主管審閱。

Transcripts, score reports, and signal-awareness comparisons from all three sessions are bundled automatically into an application packet, ready for the training manager to review.

3

培訓主管審核並核發認證

The Manager Reviews and Certifies

主管依據系統客觀報告進行審核，核發「HR 面試職能認證」，記錄於新人培訓檔案。

The manager reviews the objective report and issues an HR Interview Competency Certification, logged in the trainee's training file.

認證門檻與評分標準由各公司培訓主管自行設定，系統提供客觀數據供審核參考，不替代主管判斷。

Each company's training manager sets its own certification threshold and scoring standards. The system supplies objective data to support that review — it doesn't replace the manager's judgment.
